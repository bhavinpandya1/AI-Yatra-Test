❓ Help & FAQ

Stuck? Confused? Most answers are here.
Free AI assistants — which can I use?▶
All four major chatbots have free tiers: ChatGPT (chat.openai.com), Claude (claude.ai), Gemini (gemini.google.com), Copilot (copilot.microsoft.com). Gemini and Copilot are the most generous on the free tier in 2026. Every skill in this program transfers 1:1 across them.
Which AI should I learn first?▶
Whichever you have easiest access to — they are ~90% interchangeable. If choosing fresh: Gemini (most generous free tier, great multimodal), Copilot (works inside Microsoft Office), Claude (best long-form writing and coding nuance), or ChatGPT (broadest ecosystem and plugins). The prompting, RAG, and agent concepts you learn here apply to all of them.
I'm stuck on an exercise — what should I do?▶
1) Read the Stuck? Try this hint at the bottom of each Station Brief — it gives platform alternatives and shortcuts. 2) Skip the hands-on for now; the quiz still works on the concept alone. 3) Come back to the exercise later when you have access to the tool or more time.
Where do I save my AI Mission Journal?▶
Anywhere durable that you'll re-open: Apple Notes, Google Keep, Notion, OneNote, a Word doc, or a plain .md file. The point is having a single growing artifact of your learnings over 21 days. Don't scatter notes across 5 apps — pick one.
AI responses feel low quality. What am I doing wrong?▶
Usually under-specified prompts. Apply CRISP (Day 5): Context, Role, Input, Steps, Parameters. Also: try a more capable model (e.g. Claude Opus or GPT-5 instead of mini/Haiku/Flash) for complex tasks. If it's a factual task, switch to Perplexity or any AI with web search enabled.
Can I do multiple missions per day to catch up?▶
Yes, but max 2/day. The 30-minute-a-day pace is intentional — retention drops sharply with cramming. If you must catch up, do two missions with a break between them, not back-to-back.
I lost my progress when I switched browsers or devices. Why?▶
Progress saves in your browser's local storage — specific to that browser on that device. To keep one consistent progress: use ONE device, ONE browser. There is no cloud sync (which keeps the app fully private).
Is there an official certificate?▶
This app gives you the skills of a certified AI Expert, not an official paper certificate. For paper credentials, pair this with: Google AI Essentials (Coursera, ~$49), Microsoft AI Fundamentals (AI-900), or AWS Certified AI Practitioner. All three are valuable for resumes.
How do I move from learning to actual consulting?▶
Days 20–21 cover this. Short version: build the Pitch Pack (Day 21) → send to 10 people in your network → take ANY first paid engagement (even ₹25K) → do excellent work → ask for referrals. Most successful AI consultants got their first client within 2 weeks of finishing this kind of program.
AWS vs Azure vs Google Cloud for AI — which to learn?▶
All three offer the same building blocks: hosted LLMs (AWS Bedrock, Azure OpenAI, Google Vertex AI), compute, and storage. Pick based on what your client already uses. The AI skills transfer between them — only the dashboards differ. As a consultant, learn to speak fluently about all three rather than mastering one.
I'm a senior leader — is this program right for me?▶
Yes. Skim Days 1–6 (you may know some basics), focus on Days 8–14 (hands-on capability), and concentrate on Days 15–21 (strategy, governance, change management, pricing). The strategy week is where leaders extract the most value.
100% free path through the whole program — is it possible?▶
Absolutely. Use only: Gemini (chat, free), Microsoft Copilot (free with MS account), NotebookLM (free), Make.com (free tier), Hugging Face (free), Google AI Studio (free API playground). You won't be missing meaningful capability versus paid tiers for the purposes of this course.
